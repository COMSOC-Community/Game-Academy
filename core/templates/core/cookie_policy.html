The Game Academy ("we," "us," "our") is committed to make fair use of the information you share with us. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies on our website game-academy.org (the "Platform"). By using our platform, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences, login status, or security settings.

2. What Cookies Do We Use?

The Game Academy only makes use of necessary cookies. These cookies are essential for the operation of the platform. They enable basic functions like security, page navigation, and form submission. These cookies do not require your consent.

Specifically, we use the following necessary cookies:

  • csrftoken: This cookie is set by the Django framework to help protect the site against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks. It is essential for security and is stored for 1 year.
  • rc::a and rc::c: These cookies are set by Google reCAPTCHA to identify bots and protect the site from malicious spam attacks. The "rc::a" cookie is persistent, while "rc::c" is a session cookie that expires when you end your browsing session.
  • cookie-consent: This cookie stores the user's consent for accepting the cookie policy. It is set when the user clicks the "Accept" button on the cookie consent banner and expires after 100 days.

3. Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the proper functionality and security of the platform.
  • To protect the platform against malicious activities (e.g., spam, bots).

4. Cookie Consent

By using our platform, you consent to the use of cookies as outlined in this policy. You are not required to give explicit consent for necessary cookies, as they are essential for the platform to function properly. However, you can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. How to Manage or Disable Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your web browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on the platform.

To manage cookies in popular browsers, you can refer to the following links:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Apple Safari
